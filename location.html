Concord

The hombu dojo (home dojo) is located in Concord which was founded in 1984. Andrew Roubas Shihan is the chief instructor of the hombu dojo in Concord and regularly teaches classes. We are blessed to have regular tuition from such an experienced 8th Dan instructor. The training is vigorous and spirited to reflect traditional training as found in Japan. There is a large contingent of Black Belts that train at this dojo providing a variety of teaching styles for all grade levels.

Dojo Address: Strathfield North Public School, enter from Cumming Ave

Carlingford

The Carford dojo was founded in 2010 under the guidance of Stefan Roubas Shihan. This dojo is heavily influenced by fresh and innovative training methods inspired by Jayson Cook Shihan (5th Dan) and Nigel Hook Sensei (3rd Dan). This dojo currently has a strong teenage presence with high ranking Black & Brown Belts.

Dojo Address: 543 North Rocks Road, Carlingford
